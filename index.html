About Our Farm

Our Home in Aurangabad

Nestled in the serene countryside of Aurangabad, Maharashtra, our farm provides the perfect environment for our goats to thrive naturally.

We believe in ethical farming practices where our goats are exclusively grass and grain-fed, ensuring the milk they produce is of the highest quality and nutritional value. Each goat is cared for with attention and respect, allowing them to live stress-free lives in open pastures.

Our Process

From Farm to Your Doorstep

Our milk production process begins with hand extraction, a traditional method that ensures gentle handling and maintains the milk's natural properties.

After collection, the milk is immediately stored in specialized ice boxes to preserve its freshness and nutritional value. It is then transported to our warehouse in Vasai, where it undergoes quality checks before being packaged and delivered to you, maintaining the farm-fresh quality throughout the journey.

Our Goal

Committed to Natural Living

At Ummid Goat Farm, our mission is to provide natural, additive-free dairy products that contribute to healthier living for our customers.

We believe that what nature provides is perfect as it is. That's why we never add preservatives, artificial flavors, or unnecessary processing to our products. Our goal is to bring you the goodness of goat milk just as nature intended – pure, nutritious, and delicious.
